Revenue Management - Job Description

About Hospitality Minds

Hospitality Minds is a Hotel Marketing company initiated in venturing into the mid-market segment hotels in the Indian hospitality perspective.Hospitality Minds Being one of the Top 10 Hospitality consulting firms in India today offers its services to major hotels across the country in various destinations like Mumbai, Darjeeling, Gangtok, Igatpuri, Shirdi, Mahabaleshwar, Gurgoan, Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Goa.

Role

Revenue Manager

Job Location

Mumbai

Core Job Responsibilities and & Duties

  • Look after the setup of the hotels for content - rates and offers on all OTA's
  • Thorough check on competitors and there movements.
  • Deal with OTA's like MMT, Goibibo, booking.com and many for better peformance of hotels.
  • Maintain a better online reputation of hotels.
  • Look after building an online presence on new online portals.
  • Grow online revenue for portals.
  • Solve issues of Hotels online and offline.
  • Work on rates and inventory management and better the yield per inventory.
  • Look after the growth of hotel in sales and reviews.
  • Represent hotel in seminars and explore new avenues for business development.
  • Travel and audit the hotel on regular basis.
