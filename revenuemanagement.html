How to increase my online sales?
  • Maintain Rate Parity
  • Ensure you display the best of the images on all the sites
  • Make sure you have more and more positive reviews on the sites
Can we reject any online booking?
The answer is a Big No, because it may lead to removal of your coupon codes or some other OTA’s may charge you penalty twice the amount. To be on the safer side ensure you have distributed your online inventory properly.
How to maintain rate parity?
  • To maintain rate parity, make sure you always a Channel Manager so that it’s easy for you to update rates from a common pool.
  • Ensure you have the same room categories and rate plans on all the OTA’s
  • Also make sure that whatever margin you are offering to one OTA’s equally should be given to all
Which Promotion or offer can produce more bookings?
Production of more booking through offers and promotions differ from Market to Market If you are located in any metro city then the pattern have shown that Last minute bookings within 72 hours will work for you and if you are located in a leisure destination then Early bird offer before 15 days or 20 days will work for you.
