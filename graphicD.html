Graphic Designer Intern - Job Description

About Hospitality Minds

Hospitality Minds is a Hotel Marketing company initiated in venturing into the mid-market segment hotels in the Indian hospitality perspective.Hospitality Minds Being one of the Top 10 Hospitality consulting firms in India today offers its services to major hotels across the country in various destinations like Mumbai, Darjeeling, Gangtok, Igatpuri, Shirdi, Mahabaleshwar, Gurgoan, Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Goa.

Role

Graphic Designer Intern

Job Location

Mumbai / WFH

Core Job Responsibilities and & Duties

  • Making logos.
  • Making creatives for hotels.
  • Coordinate with the social media marketing team.
  • Making and editing videos.
  • Work on innovating new ideas and creatives for clients.
  • Keeping a close eye on competitors' designs and creatives.
  • Understand the competitor's hotel designs and make better ones.
  • Think of unique and trendy creatives for hotels.
  • Edit digital video content for platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, etc.
  • Handle the editing of high-quality videos.
  • Work with our creative teammates.
  • Must have hands-on 2D animation, motion graphics, etc.
  • Must have a proper knowledge of video editing softwares such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, etc.
